Two Phases of Brainstorming

Divergent Thinking

Generate as many ideas as possible. Quantity first, quality later. Don't filter or evaluate -- just create.

Add Categories for Variety

Section 4 - 6:30

Four Advanced Brainstorming Techniques

1 Role-Playing

Have Claude brainstorm as different people: frustrated beginner, marketing expert, skeptic, investor.

"Brainstorm 5 features from the perspective of a frustrated beginner who keeps getting stuck."

2 Constraints

Add limits to spark creativity: budget, time, technology constraints force unconventional solutions.

"Generate 10 feature ideas that cost zero dollars to build."

3 Worst Ideas FAVORITE

Generate deliberately bad ideas, analyze why they're bad, then invert each into a good idea.

"Generate the 10 worst feature ideas. Then invert each into a good idea."

4 Combination

Take 2-3 ideas and ask Claude to merge them. Two okay ideas often combine into one great idea.

"How could I combine these three ideas into one feature?"
