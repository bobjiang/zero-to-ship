Phase 4

Iterate & Refine

This is where the magic happens. Look at what you have, identify what needs to change, and tell Claude specifically what to fix.

Expect 3-5 iterations. That's not a failure -- it's the process.

Section 6 - 12:00

Common Roadblocks (and Solutions)

⚠ Code doesn't run
Paste the error into Claude: "I'm getting this error: [error]. Can you fix it?"
⚠ Design doesn't look right
Be specific: "The header overlaps the hero section" -- describe what you see, not just "it's wrong."
⚠ Stuck for 30+ minutes
Stop. Ask the Discord community. Or tell Claude: "Let's try a completely different approach."
⚠ Feature creep
Go back to your 3-feature list. If it's not there, it's V2. Repeat: "Ship first, improve later."
