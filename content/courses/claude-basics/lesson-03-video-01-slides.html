Section 3 • 5:30

Iteration 1: The First Response

You
Write a welcome message for a Discord server for people learning to build apps with AI.
C
Welcome to our community! We're a group of developers and enthusiasts interested in building applications with artificial intelligence...
Verdict: Clear and structured, but generic. Could be for any Discord server.
Section 3 • 6:30

Iteration 2: Adding Specificity

You
This is good, but can you make it more casual and encouraging? Mention that our community is for absolute beginners building with Claude. Add a line about how everyone started where they are.
C
Hey there, welcome! So glad you're here. This is a space for absolute beginners who are learning to build real things with Claude AI. Everyone here started exactly where you are right now...
Improvement: Warmer tone, mentions beginners, encouraging. Getting closer!
Section 3 • 7:00

Iteration 3: Final Polish

You
Almost perfect. Can you add a mention of our 02Ship courses and end with a question to get new members talking?
C
Hey there, welcome to 02Ship! So glad you're here. This community is for absolute beginners who are building real projects with Claude AI. Check out our courses to get started, and remember: everyone here started exactly where you are. So, what's the first thing you want to build?
Result: On-brand, encouraging, mentions 02Ship, ends with engagement prompt!
