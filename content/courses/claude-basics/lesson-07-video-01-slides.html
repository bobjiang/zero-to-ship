What is an MVP?

Minimum
MVP
Viable

Section 1 - 2:30

MVP: Myth vs. Reality

✗ MVP does NOT mean...

Broken, embarrassing, low-quality, or half-baked. It is not an excuse to ship garbage.

✓ MVP DOES mean...

Focused. One core value proposition. Ruthlessly prioritized. Small but genuinely useful.

Section 5 - 8:30

The MVP Planning Template

Four sections. That's all you need to plan your MVP. Fill this out before you write a single line of code.

MVP Planning Template

1. Problem
What problem are you solving? Write from the user's perspective.
2. Solution
One sentence describing how you solve it.
3. Features (Max 3)
The three most important features. No more.
4. Success Criteria
How do you know it worked? 2-3 measurable things.

Section 5 - 10:00

Define Success Before You Build

Simple, measurable, focused on real users

"5 people complete at least one lesson and tell me it was helpful."
That's it. Simple. Measurable. Focused on user behavior, not vanity metrics. This becomes your north star for what to build and what to skip.
