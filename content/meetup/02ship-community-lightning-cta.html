00:00 – 05:00 (5 min)
  • 0:00 – 0:45 · Hook & mission. “02Ship exists for the people who were told they’d never code. With AI, that story is over.” Briefly share why the community was started.
  • 0:45 – 1:45 · What we offer. Walk through the four checkmarks: free learning portal, monthly meetups, builder network, curated daily AI news. Name-drop a recent meetup or lesson drop if possible.
  • 1:45 – 2:45 · Proof & momentum. Share one concrete community win — a member who shipped, a hackathon, or a notable talk. Keep it human; make it feel inevitable they could do it too.
  • 2:45 – 3:45 · The ask — lightning talks. Pivot to the QR panel. Explain what a lightning talk is: 5 minutes, one thing you shipped (or failed at) with AI. No slides required, no tech background assumed.
  • 3:45 – 4:30 · Scan the QR. Pause here. Literally ask the room to pull out phones. “Scan now, submit later — the link saves to your browser.” URL: 02ship.com/submit.
  • 4:30 – 5:00 · Close. Invite them to the next meetup date, say thanks, and leave the QR on screen for post-talk questions.
  • Props to hand out: index cards with 02ship.com/submit for anyone without a phone.
  • Fallback if no one scans: walk up with your phone, scan live, show the form on the projector.
